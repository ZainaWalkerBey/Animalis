Hammerheads are not targeted directly by commercial fisheries, hammerheads are a bycatch species of tropical longline and drift net fisheries with highly valued fins.Finning is when people take the fins off of sea creaturs and toss the bodies back into the body of water for them to die.

Finning isn't very well known, but it is actually a major cause of sea creature extinction that remains ignored.
